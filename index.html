islampur ramkrishnapally rural welfare society
"Reaching the Unreached"
“IRRWS is a movement and congregation of beautiful minds to make the world a better place to live in, to make the society free from evils of inequality and poverty.”
Our Mission
Is to aware the rural people about their Health, Education and basic rights, assists them to recognize and improve their potentialities through skill formation, knowledge sharing and transfer of technical know how, guide them to generate economic benefit for promoting sustainable development in rural economy.
Our Vision
In the 21st century IRRWS is poised to be the pioneer for resource center assuring social-orientation, economic growth, local capacity building and empowerment of progress and success. IRRWS- vision is to achieve a value-oriented sustainable society based on people for a complete social transformation.
Our Goal
to offer opportunities for personal growth and development, skill enhancement by providing technical and vocational skills
to motivate and recognize each persons efforts and achievement so that one can recognize ones own worth
to empower the poor and marginalized communities through economic and development
to provide ongoing support system and services to the target group beneficiaries through efficient and readily availably management information system
to reach out poorest and underprivileged section of the society for self reliance in health, education and economic prosperity

working for our commitments
1
To promote all round rural development including monitoring and evaluation of rural based programmes and projects.
2
To promote social, economical, health & educational development of the weaker section of the society, specially SC, ST, OBC and minority community
3
To promote formal and non-formal education ( both male & female ) , Family welfare and to look after and to attend to the acute and chronic cases of the ailing persons, community Health Education, Rural sanitation, drinking water etc
4
To promote Health & sanitation activities and establish Hospitals , Poly Clinic Diagnostic Center etc for the benefit of the rural poor people
5
To cooperate with National and International Organizations having similar objective and depute or receive representatives from such organizations and to work for development of community awareness and vocational activity for the rural mass
6
To establish training cum research center for the unemployed and in improve village economy through Agriculture, Horticulture, Bee Keeping, poultry Farming , Dairy framing , Duckery , Goatery Animal Husbandry , Hatchery , Fishery , Nursery for plants and flowers
7
To help the sick, aged and indignant persons and to provide home for the aged people, unprotected girls and women
8
To acquire, establish , start , aid, run, maintain, or manage schools, colleges, libraries, hospitals, Ayurvedic and Homeopathic center for the benefit of the people
9
To undertake different type of welfare programmes for the disabled
